About Us

SOCAM INTERNATIONAL LIMITED is a professional electronic components distributor headquartered in Hong Kong. We specialize in the agency and distribution of high-quality semiconductor products, serving customers across Asia and beyond.

As an authorized distributor for leading Chinese semiconductor brands, we bridge the gap between top-tier manufacturers and customers worldwide. Our commitment to reliability, competitive pricing, and excellent service makes us the preferred partner for businesses seeking quality electronic components.

Our product portfolio covers analog ICs, video surveillance chips, security MCUs, and general-purpose microcontrollers — supporting diverse applications in consumer electronics, industrial automation, security systems, and IoT.
